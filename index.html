Welcome to LFG. What is this place?

The name's pretty suggestive, honestly. If you're a gamer, need to find other gamers to game with, you're at the right place.

I get it. How do I use this thing?

Simple. Just register, help us out with a bit of data about your league stats and username, and we'll find a duo for you!

Frequently asked questions

Someone lied about their rank or username. What can I do?

Until we will implement a system that can verify your league account and automatically find its rank without needing to enter it yourself, you can report these accounts and we will check them manually(since we do not have many active users yet, as this site is in early stages of development).

If I find a player I would like to queue up with, how do I contact them if there is no chat function in the site?

Just add them in-game! We doubt they'll be checking the website unless they're browsing for people to play with at that exact moment anyway.
